48956: Staircase endpoint identifiers for skool region 191
Used by the routine at 25700. Skool region 191 corresponds to anywhere on the bottom floor (see the skool region data table at 47420) or in mid-air (see 46908 and 47164).
Each entry in this table determines the first staircase endpoint to reach on the way from skool region 191 to the destination region (189-196). Region 192 is the assembly hall stage (see 28067).
