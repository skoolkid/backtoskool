53797: Lesson descriptors
When a lesson starts, the routine at 63309 picks up the lesson descriptor from this table and copies it into 32736. Each entry in this table defines the teacher for the period (if any), and the room. The teacher is identified by bits 4-7:
Bits Teacher
0000 None
0001 MR WACKER
0010 MR WITHIT
0011 MR ROCKITT
0100 MR CREAK
The room is identified by bits 0-3:
Bits Room
0010 PLAYTIME
0011 ASSEMBLY
0100 DINNER
0101 REVISION LIBRARY
0110 SCIENCE LAB
0111 BLUE ROOM
1000 YELLOW ROOM
