BD3C: Staircase endpoint identifiers for skool region 0xBD
Used by the routine at 6464. Skool region 0xBD is on the top floor of the boys' skool, anywhere to the left of the left study door (x<=72). It is defined by the first entry in the skool region data table at B73C.
Each entry in this table determines the first staircase endpoint to reach on the way from skool region 0xBD to the destination region (0xBD-0xC4). Region 0xBF corresponds to anywhere on the bottom floor (see B93C) or in mid-air (see B73C and B83C); region 0xC0 is the assembly hall stage (see 6DA3).
