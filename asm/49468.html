49468: Staircase endpoint identifiers for skool region 193
Used by the routine at 25700. Skool region 193 is on the middle floor, to the left of the window in the boys' skool (76<x<=96). It is defined by the third entry in the skool region data table at 47164.
Each entry in this table determines the first staircase endpoint to reach on the way from skool region 193 to the destination region (189-196). Region 191 corresponds to anywhere on the bottom floor (see 47420) or in mid-air (see 46908 and 47164); region 192 is the assembly hall stage (see 28067).
