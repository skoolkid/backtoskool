BD44: Data for staircase endpoint 0xBD
Used by the routines at 6464 and 64F3. Staircase endpoint 0xBD is the top of the staircase leading down from the Revision Library.
Input
Output
BD44 DEFB $1A x-coordinate of the top of the staircase
BD45 DEFB $00 Face left to descend the staircase
BD46 DEFB $4D LSB of 644D (descend staircase)
