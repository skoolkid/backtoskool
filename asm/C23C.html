C23C: Staircase endpoint identifiers for skool region 0xC2
Used by the routine at 6464. Skool region 0xC2 is on the top floor, to the right of the left study door (72<x<=96). It is defined by the second entry in the skool region data table at B73C.
Each entry in this table determines the first staircase endpoint to reach on the way from skool region 0xC2 to the destination region (0xBD-0xC4). Region 0xBF corresponds to anywhere on the bottom floor (see B93C) or in mid-air (see B73C and B83C); region 0xC0 is the assembly hall stage (see 6DA3).
