49724: Staircase endpoint identifiers for skool region 194
Used by the routine at 25700. Skool region 194 is on the top floor, to the right of the left study door (72<x<=96). It is defined by the second entry in the skool region data table at 46908.
Each entry in this table determines the first staircase endpoint to reach on the way from skool region 194 to the destination region (189-196). Region 191 corresponds to anywhere on the bottom floor (see 47420) or in mid-air (see 46908 and 47164); region 192 is the assembly hall stage (see 28067).
