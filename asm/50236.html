50236: Staircase endpoint identifiers for skool region 196
Used by the routine at 25700. Skool region 196 is the top floor in the girls' skool. It is defined by the fourth entry in the skool region data table at 46908.
Each entry in this table determines the first staircase endpoint to reach on the way from skool region 196 to the destination region (189-196). Region 191 corresponds to anywhere on the bottom floor (see 47420) or in mid-air (see 46908 and 47164); region 192 is the assembly hall stage (see 28067).
