BF44: Data for staircase endpoint 0xBF
Used by the routines at 6464 and 64F3. Staircase endpoint 0xBF is the top of the staircase leading down to the bottom floor on the far left of the boys' skool.
Input
Output
BF44 DEFB $0F x-coordinate of the top of the staircase
BF45 DEFB $80 Face right to descend the staircase
BF46 DEFB $4D LSB of 644D (descend staircase)
BF47 DEFB $08 Number of steps in the staircase
