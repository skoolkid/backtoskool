CE44: Data for staircase endpoint 0xCE
Used by the routines at 6464 and 64F3. Staircase endpoint 0xCE is the bottom of the staircase leading up to the top floor in the girls' skool.
CE44 DEFB $BA x-coordinate of the bottom of the staircase
CE45 DEFB $00 Face left to ascend the staircase
CE46 DEFB $38 LSB of 6438 (ascend staircase)
CE47 DEFB $08 Number of steps in the staircase
