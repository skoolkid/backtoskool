D437: Blackboard identifiers
Used by the routine at 6755. Each identifier is the LSB of the corresponding blackboard buffer. The x-coordinates of the left edges of the blackboards can be found at D337, and the y-coordinates of the top rows of the blackboards can be found at D537.
