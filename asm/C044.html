C044: Data for staircase endpoint 0xC0
Used by the routines at 6464 and 64F3. Staircase endpoint 0xC0 is the bottom of the staircase leading up to the middle floor on the far left of the boys' skool.
C044 DEFB $16 x-coordinate of the bottom of the staircase
C045 DEFB $00 Face left to ascend the staircase
C046 DEFB $38 LSB of 6438 (ascend staircase)
C047 DEFB $08 Number of steps in the staircase
