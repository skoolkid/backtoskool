F02F: Make a teacher give lines to EINSTEIN or the kid he grassed up
Used by the routine at F100. Makes a teacher give lines to EINSTEIN (for telling tales) 84 times out of 256, or give lines to the kid EINSTEIN grassed up (if any) the rest of the time.
Input
B Reprimand message number
C Character number of the kid to give lines to (206, 208 or 210)
H Teacher's character number (201-203)
Output
